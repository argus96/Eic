collage canteen

EIC features a spacious and hygienic canteen offering a wide variety of nutritious meals, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices. The canteen provides a comfortable, well-maintained dining environment where students can take a break, relax, and enjoy fresh, quality food. It serves as an ideal space for socializing, group discussions, or simply recharging between classes. The facility emphasizes cleanliness and promotes healthy eating habits, ensuring a pleasant and welcoming atmosphere for students, faculty, and staff alike. It is an important hub for interaction and refreshment on campus.

collage building

The EIC building is a state-of-the-art facility designed to foster a dynamic learning atmosphere. With spacious classrooms, modern lecture halls, and specialized labs for practical training, it offers a perfect balance of functionality and comfort. The building is equipped with advanced technology to support teaching and learning, while large windows and open spaces ensure a bright and airy environment. It also includes student lounges, faculty offices, and recreational areas, providing a comprehensive space for academic, social, and extracurricular activities, making it a vibrant hub for students and staff alike
