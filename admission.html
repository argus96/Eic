Scholarship Details

We offer a variety of scholarships to support students based on merit, financial need, and special achievements.

  • Merit-Based: Awarded to students with outstanding academic performance.
  • Need-Based: Designed for students requiring financial assistance.
  • Research Grants: Available for students engaged in innovative research projects.
  • Corporate Sponsorships: Offered in collaboration with industry leaders.
